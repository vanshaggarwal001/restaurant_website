Our Story

Cooking like
nobody's watching the clock.

Olive & Ember started as a single wood-fired oven and a borrowed dining room. The idea never changed: cook what's in season, pour what pairs, and let the evening run at its own pace.

Our chefs work from a market list that's rewritten every morning, which means the menu shifts gently through the year — but the things people love, the slow-roasted lamb, the burnt-honey tart, never really leave.
